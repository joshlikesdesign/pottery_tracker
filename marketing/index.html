Pottery Tracker Pro

When your studio outgrows the basics

Free covers the essentials — up to 40 active pieces and full photo-first tracking. Pro unlocks unlimited pieces, kiln logging, analytics, exports, batch tools, trim reminders, templates, and optional iCloud sync across your Apple devices.

Pricing & purchase

$2.99/mo · $29.99/yr (~$2.50/mo)

Subscriptions are an in-app purchase on iPhone and iPad. In Pottery Tracker, open Settings and find the Pottery Tracker Pro section — or tap any feature marked Pro to see plans.

The yearly plan often includes a 7-day free trial where Apple offers it. Pricing varies by country and may change; the App Store always shows current local price.

Get the app & upgrade

Cancel or manage your subscription anytime in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Restoring purchases works from the paywall if you reinstall.
